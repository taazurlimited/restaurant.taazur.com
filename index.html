    Catering & Food Supply

    Established thirty years ago, today our catering division continues to prosper and grow as regularly undertakes a variety of industrial and commercial catering projects that often require high quality and professional culinary services providing a variety of nutritionally balanced menus, which are planned by our professional chefs for a diverse range of ethnicities. Our catering management and staff are highly experienced and have been carefully selected from the food and beverage industry. Each member of our team brings attributes to our organization that are unsurpassed. Catering mission is to provide catering services with the most stringent standards of quality control and hygiene. All of our food products and ingredients are of approved quality as per the standards of the Saudi Health Department and international food hygiene and safety standards. We are ISO 22000 certified, which is HACCAP & food safety quality management system. Our Health & Safety Officers and Hygiene Inspectors are continuously involved in ensuring all hygiene standards are maintained at the highest levels throughout our kitchens and other catering-related areas. currently serves over 10,000 meals per day in the Eastern Province alone.
